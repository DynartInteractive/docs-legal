## What Information Do We Collect?

### 1.1 Information You Give Us

We receive and store information that you provide, for example when you:

* download, update, or play our games
* enter competitions or events
* participate in special activities (such as application testing)
* interact with us via social media (Facebook, Twitter, Twitch, YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, Discord, Slack)
* enter information on our websites
* purchase products
* subscribe to newsletters or information feeds
* make in-app purchases
* buy items in our online store
* request technical support or contact us in any way

---

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain types of information when you interact with us, such as:

* how you use our Services (to design new Services and improve existing ones)
* basic profile information from Game Center, Google Play, and other platforms (name, age range, profile photo, friends list)
* information about your device (make & model, OS version, platform, language, time zone, carrier, installed apps, IP address)
* cookies (you may disable cookies, but some features may not work)
* your device’s advertising ID (you may reset it at any time)
* general location (city/country based on IP) to adjust language and regional content
* any information provided when using contact forms or similar features

---

### 1.3 Information From Third Parties

We may collect information about you from third parties when necessary to provide Services you request:

* purchases made via the App Store, Google Play, or other storefronts
* logging in through a social media account (e.g., Facebook) — we may receive your name, profile picture, age range, and friends list
* using in-app features such as Game Center — we may receive your username and gameplay information

## How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

### To let you play our games

We work with vendors and partners who provide the technical resources necessary for our games to function.

### For product support, development, and improvement

We use personal data to provide customer support, develop new games, improve existing ones, and conduct playtesting and market research.

### To provide advertisements

We may show advertisements in our games or on related platforms. Some ads may be tailored to your interests.

### To market our products

We may send you marketing messages via email, social media, push notifications, and other channels. You may opt out at any time.

### To perform analytics

We analyze data to understand how our Services are used, improve performance, and make recommendations.
We may combine data from multiple sources.

## 2.5 Marketing, In-App Purchases & Opting-Out

### 2.5.1 Direct Marketing

We may send you updates about new games, events, or merchandise.
You may opt out at any time.

### 2.5.2 In-App Purchases

Some games offer paid features or items.
If you do not want to make purchases, avoid paid features or disable in-app purchases on your device.

### 2.5.3 Advertising ID

Advertising IDs help tailor your in-game experience.

**Apple iOS:**
`Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier`

**Android:**
`Google Settings → Ads → Reset Advertising ID`

### 2.5.4 Opting Out

Ways to opt out:

* click “unsubscribe” in any email we send
* “unlike” our Facebook page
* unfollow us on Twitter or other platforms
* use opt-out settings in third-party services

For help, email **[info@dynart.net](mailto:info@dynart.net)**.
